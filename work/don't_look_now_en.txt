<h1>Don't look now (2012)</h1>
<p>Music/Sound design</p>
<br>
<p><em>Don't look now</em> is a book installation by <a href="http://editiontaube.de/" target="_blank">Edition Taube</a>. A grainy audio loop, working with sounds produced by books, creates the sonic ambiance.</p>
