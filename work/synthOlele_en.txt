<h1>synthOlele (2012)</h1>
<p>manufacturing of instrument and electronic live music</p>
<br>
<p>The synthOlele is a digital instrument. 
<br>
It combines several elements for the control of synthesized sound hence working as an interface. There is a certain resemblance when you look at it next to a traditional ukulele. This is not a coincidence but strict plagiarism aiming for the synthOlele to become the ukuleles' electronic cover version.</p>
<br>
<p>The synthOlele was developed in collaboration with Dominik Noli. Und als Projekt am Institut Fuer Musik und Medien...</p>
<br>
<p>Concerts:
<br>
02/2012 Monkey's Club, D&uuml;sseldorf (club version) 
<br>
02/2012 Cit&eacute; International des Arts, Paris (experimental version)  </p>
