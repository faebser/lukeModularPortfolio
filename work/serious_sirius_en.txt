<h1>Serious Sirius – Improvisation Game (2011)</h1>
<p>Live electronics</p>
<br>
<p>This improvisational game follows a simple set of rules inspired by the methods of John Zorn, who was influenced by Anthony Braxton, who taught Philipp Schulze (who guided Serious Sirius at Institut Fuer Musik und Medien).<br>
Violin: Mischa Tangian, contrabass: Richard Eisenach, drums: Stephan Vester, elektronics: Lukas Truniger</p>
<br>
<h1>Concert</h1>
<p>06/2011 vernissage of the exhibition <em>Monte Verit&agrave;</em>, Kunstverein Familie Montez, Frankfurt</p>
