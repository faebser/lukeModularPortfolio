<h1>Improvisation with Prepared Consumer Electronics (2012)</h1>
<a href="https://vimeo.com/41116911">Improvisation with Prepared Consumer Electronics</a>
<p>live elektronics</p>
<br>
<p>For this performance electrical goods produced for entertainment purposes are found, prepared and used as instruments. The Lo-Fi-technology allows a kind of improvisation that can be themed &bdquo;bugs are features&ldquo;. The contrastive clean visuals based on graphic algorithms are created by Jan Steinbach.</p>
<br>
<p>Performances:
<br>
02/2012 Cit&eacute; International des Arts, Paris
<br>
03/2012 Dimanche Rouge, Divan du Monde, Paris</p>
