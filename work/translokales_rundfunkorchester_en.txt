<h1>Translokales Rundfunkorchester (since 2010)</h1>
<p>Live electronics</p>
<br>
<p>An experimental ensemble for manipulated radios. Through the search for a new voice for an old fashioned medium, the radio program becomes an instrument. We combine media techniques from two centuries: radiophonic information transfer and state of the art digital media technologies.<br>
The Translocal Rundfunk Orchestra was founded in 2009 in the Music Informatics major at Institut Fuer Musik Und Medien and plays in various orchestrations.<br>
<a href="http://rundfunkorchester.blogsport.eu/">Translokales Rundfunkorchester</a>
<br>
<b>Members</b><br>
Jonas Hummel, Maurice Braun, Hannes H&ouml;lzl, Julius Hochstrate, Lukas Truniger. Additionally there is a changing cast of guests.
</p>
