<p>Reliable news is spread by gossip and carrier pigeons provide the smallest data carrier, then fed to  <a href="#i_never_wrote_a_sound">mechanical computers</a> built and used in an <a href="#die_theorie_des_himmels">auditory space</a> (they are operated via bucket drums outputting machine code). To this world rhythms are vital, the rhythm of speech, of thought, of movement and <a href="#dance">dance</a>. Not only do they carry information, they are information, potential energy that informs each and every body.</p>
