<p>Microscopic and macroscopic structures of urban sound (work in progress)
<br><br>
As an approach to the microscopic spheres of urban sound I work towards carving out grains of the cities' noises using contact microphones. And via time lapse I perceptualize macroscopic structures, rhythms and single notes that extend over hours and days.</p>
