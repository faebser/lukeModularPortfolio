<h1>Microscopic and macroscopic structures of urban sound (work in progress 2012)</h1>
<p>As an approach to the microscopic spheres of urban sound, I work towards carving out grains of the city's noises using contact microphones. Via time lapse, I perceptualize macroscopic structures, rhythms and single notes that extend over hours and days.</p>
